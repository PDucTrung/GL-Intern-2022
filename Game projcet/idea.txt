#Game name: Super shark
* Background: Bringing in curiosity the baby shark loves to explore the ocean, help him overcome the obstacles to roam the ocean.
* Genre: adventure, arcade
* Operation mechanism: To control, press SPACEBAR to jump avoiding the obstacles on the way.
                       Overcome the obstacles to score points.
* Goal: To survive as long as possible.
* Features: Simple gameplay but difficult to master.
